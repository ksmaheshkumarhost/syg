    Interior design describes a group of various yet related projects that involve turning an interior space into an "effective setting for the range of human activities" that are to take place there.

    Synergy Décor, a budding interior design firm, is based out of Chennai. With a team of young and creative minds to provide innovative design options to customers, ably guided and executed by well experienced professionals, we are specialized in interior designing of Office & Residence. We provide customized solutions on space planning, layouts, partitions, wardrobes and modular kitchens.

    We emphasize on quality of work, timely completion of projects to the fullest satisfaction of our clients. We focus on providing a complete solution to our customers, by also undertaking civil and electrical works related to interiors.
